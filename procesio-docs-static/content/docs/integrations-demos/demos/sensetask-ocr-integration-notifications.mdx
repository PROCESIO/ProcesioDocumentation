SenseTask uses AI and OCR (optical character recognition) to automate data entry from paper-based documents.&#x20;

In this use case we'll show you how to use data from SenseTask using [webhooks](https://docs.procesio.com/webhooks) in a workflow that sends notifications.

### **Prerequisites**

- Procesio account
- SenseTask account
- Smartbill account (optional)

### **Webhook**

&#x20; **Step 1**:    In your [Procesio](https://procesio.com/) account navigate to **Automation**.

&#x20; **Step 2**:    Select **Webhooks** and click **Create Webhook.**

![](/images/docs/GUh3p2-Xn7VjV8tHsby6i_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 3**:    Give your webhook a name and copy the link from **Webhook url\***.

![](/images/docs/7Jena7gIUH2rWqCjMBUwi_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 4**:    Open a new tab and navigate to your [Sensetask account](https://sensetask.com/).

&#x20; **Step 5**:    Click the **Settings&#x20;**&#x77;idget located in the upper right corner.

![](/images/docs/5XKNnU8MyT7hNYIGoWb7C_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 6**:    Expand the **Developers&#x20;**&#x64;ropdown and select Webhooks.

![](/images/docs/rcHX7AARq7WL9NQHqeEYu_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 7**:    Click **Create new webhook&#x20;**&#x61;nd in the popup enter:

- **Description&#x20;**- give it a description/name.
- **Endpoint URL** - paste the **Webhook Url&#x20;**&#x73;aved in Step 3.
- **Select events to listen to** - choose the option that best suits what you need.

![](/images/docs/UvpGcrWUgcKBFAnf55Km5_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 8**:    Click **Create**.

&#x20; **Step 9**:    Switch back to Procesio and click **Start listening&#x20;**(this will listen by default for a maximum of 5 minutes).

&#x20; **Step 10**:   Drag the invoice in the SenseTask **Files** section (for this demo we used an invoice created with Smartbill) and click **Start uploading and processing.&#x20;**&#x41;fter the file is processed click **Review all&#x20;**&#x66;rom the upper right corner.

![](/images/docs/6hi-EDq9YtEDE2u3FMEwp_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 11**:    Navigate back to Procesio to see the result received by the webhook.

![](/images/docs/NVhjd-dzd0kAFUR3L7K3u_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 12**: Click **Save**.

### **Create the process**

&#x20; **Step 1**:    In your Procesio account navigate to the **Process Designer** section and click **Create new**.

&#x20; **Step 2**:    Give your process a name.

&#x20; **Step 3**:    Click the **Webhooks&#x20;**&#x77;idget located in the upper right corner.

![](/images/docs/Pkj9j81JP0eAXStX8ujc4_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 4**:     Add the configurations and click **Save**:

- **Select Webhook\*** - select the webhook we created in the previous section.
- Add one or more rule to allow decide if you want to allow the webhook to run the process. In our example I just checked if a certain **node is not empty**.

![](/images/docs/tYlSaytbVbi34IceGOBPl_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 5**:    Create the variables we will use in our process:

- **SenseTaskNotification&#x20;**- was created by the webhook and it contains the data we receive from SenseTask.
- **dateRegistered&#x20;**- is a DateTime variable.
- **Month&#x20;**- is a integer variable.

![](/images/docs/pe3AAGhigLILqOLGnEf2L_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 5**:    Drag the following processes on the canvas and configure them.

![](/images/docs/mZEsbGTL_aCIVPrSqXddz_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 6**:    The **Today** action.

We will use the &lt;%dateRegistered%&gt; variable to store the current date.&#x20;

![](/images/docs/UowQ7yGB7xvD_zT9halIy_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 7**:    The **Get Month** action.

We will use the value of the &lt;%dateRegistered%&gt; variable to get the month the webhook data was received and store it in the &lt;%Month%&gt; variable.

![](/images/docs/_PzkdleTRZniCBf4_hpTf_image.png)

&#x20; **Step 8**:    The **Send Email** action.

Select the [SMTP server credential](https://docs.procesio.com/smtp-server-credential) that will be used click the **Send Email&#x20;**&#x62;utton.

We will add the &lt;%Month%&gt; variable in the subject to help the recipient group them by month and in the body we will use the insert key to add the the &lt;%SensTskNotification%&gt; to add the data inside the email in text form.

![](/images/docs/ydPeCnah9iedVsshfc_2h_image.png)

<Callout type="info">
The email will be sent to the email address you specified in the **To\*&#x20;**&#x69;nput text area.
</Callout>

**&#x20;   Step 9**:    **Save&#x20;**&#x61;nd **Validate&#x20;**&#x74;he process.

**&#x20;   Step 10**:    Add another bill to your SenseTask account.

&#x20;   **Step 11**:     Check your email account.

![](/images/docs/o4K7KmAgewRJSjj7SoOf8_image.png)
