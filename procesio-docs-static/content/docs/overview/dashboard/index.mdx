Welcome to **PROCESIO** 👋🏼

When logging into your [PROCESIO](https://procesio.app/) account you are redirected to the **Dashboard** page. The Dashboard provides information on your account and access to other sections of the platform.

![](/images/docs/4llcqO0ZHU3pjU-KIEN5o_dash.png)

There are quite a few sections in the **Dashboard&#x20;**&#x73;o let's go over each of them to see what they do:

**Workspace switch**

- This switch can be used to set the workspace that you want to work on.
- Workspaces help you manage different teams and better understand each team's resource usage.

![](/images/docs/jKIRp8gmM3j_H5Bi9Arg5_switch1.png)

- From here you can also access the **administration view** for every **master workspace** that you own.

![](/images/docs/SRQFWGO-pAzZJp5bstmcF_switch2.png)

**Navigation**

- As the name suggests, this section is used to navigate **PROCESIO**.&#x20;
- The highlighted section is the one you're currently on.

![](/images/docs/PhEeMOPUl9DzFDIcr6rnD_tdy.png)

- You can find more tools by clicking on the **More tools** button.

![](/images/docs/uIEXHPuENHbc3yveT2Xh7_tdy.png)



**Notification center**

- Here you will receive notifications regarding workspace administration and resource management.
- You will also be notified by **PROCESIO** if any actions are decommissioned.

![](/images/docs/rWy2o8y7FkjiR0byF7EN-_tdy.png)

**Discovery section**

- This section is where you learn more about **PROCESIO** and what you can do with it.
- New features will also be showcased here first to ensure you're always up to date.

![](/images/docs/oWkTomGEIme64HHJdRVbr_tdy.png)

**Documentation section**

- Here you can find links to our [main documentation](https://docs.procesio.com/).
- If you have any questions, make sure to check out the [FAQ](https://docs.procesio.com/faq) page first.

![](/images/docs/eaODtHkokQX2jMrm-AvnB_tdy.png)

**Resource Management section**

- Here you can find information about your ongoing plan and resource usage per month.
- From here you can also request a plan upgrade.

![](/images/docs/McdTpJ_LGZdsFWeX0sE4x_tdy.png)

**Community section**

- Here you can find quick links to our Discord server.
- You can also invite your friends to join **PROCESIO** from here

![](/images/docs/Ly712kG_idA5QYzvVQKKo_tdy.png)
