The Themes feature in Formbuilder by PROCESIO allows you to customize the appearance of your forms, ensuring a cohesive and visually appealing user experience. With a comprehensive set of properties ranging from colors to fonts and form elements, you can tailor the look and feel of your forms to match your brand identity or design preferences.

![](/images/docs/VugxT7vxUFFImH260-CCa_image.png)

**Colors:**

This comprehensive set of theme properties empowers you to craft visually stunning forms that align perfectly with your brand's aesthetic. Experiment with different combinations to create the perfect look for your forms. You can add your own color codes to further customize your forms and you can always use the refresh button next to each color to restore the default value.&#x20;
