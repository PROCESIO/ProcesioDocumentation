This action returns the week number for a <span style={{color:'#ff9307'}}>DateTime</span>.&#x20;

**Obs:** The week number indicates where the week falls numerically within a year.



![](/images/docs/vEicfs6FML4ql8QOLhXTy_image.png)

## Inputs & Outputs

- **\[ I ] Date**: <span style={{color:'#ff9307'}}>DateTime</span> for which to get the week number.
- **\[ I ] First week rule:** <span style={{color:'#00ced1'}}>Drop-down</span>
  - **First Day** ➜ Indicates that the first week of the year starts on the first day of the
    &#x20;year and ends before the following designated first day of the week.
  - **First Full Week** ➜ Indicates that the first week of the year is the first week with four or more days before the designated first day of the week.
  - **First Four Day Week&#x20;**➜ Indicates that the first week of the year begins on the first occurrence
    &#x20;of the designated first day of the week on or after the first day of the year.
- **\[ I ] First day of week:** <span style={{color:'#00ced1'}}>Drop-down</span>
  - **Sunday&#x20;**➜ Uses Sunday as first day of the week.
  - **Monday&#x20;**➜ Used Monday as first day of the week.
- **\[ O ] Week number**: <span style={{color:'#0039e3'}}>Integer</span> representing the extracted week number.
